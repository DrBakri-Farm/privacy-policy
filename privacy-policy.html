Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 10, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the DrBakri Farm mobile application ("App") operated by DrBakri Farm.

Information We Collect

The App requests access to the following device features:

  • Camera — Used to capture photos and scan codes as part of farm management features.
  • Location (GPS) — Used to record the GPS coordinates of trees and farm areas at the time of registration. This information is stored on our servers and associated with your farm records.
  • NFC — Used to read NFC tags attached to trees or farm equipment for identification purposes.

How We Use the Information

Data collected through the App is used solely to provide farm management features, including registering trees, logging harvests, and identifying tagged items. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Data Storage

Information collected is transmitted securely to our servers and stored for the purpose of managing your farm records.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data by contacting us. You can also revoke camera, location, or NFC permissions at any time through your device settings.
